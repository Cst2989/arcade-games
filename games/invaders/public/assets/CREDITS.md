## Sound effects

The 14 WAV files under `sfx/` are placeholder silent stubs committed as
a temporary measure so that `Sfx.load()` will not 404 when audio is
wired up. Replace with real CC0 effects from kenney.nl (Interface Sounds
pack, Impact Sounds pack, Sci-Fi Sounds pack) or freesound.org before
any public release.
